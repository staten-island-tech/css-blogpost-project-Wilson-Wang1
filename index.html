Keyboards

Mechanical Keyboards

Mechanical Keyboards are the classic keyboards that everyone should have. The switches are pleasant sounding and there are many good mechanical keyboards out there. Certain good ones include the HyperX Origin and the Steelseries Apex Pro.

Membrane Keyboards

Membrane keyboards are often used in place of mechanical keyboards. It is a cheaper option and for people who don't like the clicking sound of Mechanical keyboard, it is for them.

Custom Keyboards

Custom keyboards can be either mechanical or membrane. Most custom keyboards are Mechanical and you can choose the designs on them. You can also swap out keys and switches to what you prefer. You could buy a good custom mechanical keyboard or build one for around ~$200 or more if you desire a more premium version.
